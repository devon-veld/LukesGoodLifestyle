Meet Luke
Hello my friends
I'm Luke, a personal trainer, wellness educator and creator of the Good Gold formula. A small farm kid who moved to the city chasing more than just muscle gains.
Growing up I was always active, but it wasn't long before I became obsessed with the science behind training and the feeling of pushing past what I thought was possible. These days it's not about my own progress anymore. Helping you hit your goals, strength, mobility, energy, or just feeling good in your body again, is where the real joy is.
I'm big on:
✓ Evidence-based strength & hypertrophy training, no bro-science
✓ Conditioning, mobility & injury prevention for life
✓ Functional strength that helps you in everyday life
✓ Real relationships, not a transaction, a transformation
✓ Fitness that's fun, sustainable and genuinely enjoyable
✓ Supporting your mental health through movement
Ready to feel strong, confident and energised again? I've got you.
